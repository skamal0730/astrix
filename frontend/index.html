Xyther: Intent-Based Trading Infrastructure

Users sign declarative intents. Solvers compete to execute best routes. Settlement is verifiable on-chain.

Open Live Demo Investor Snapshot

Problem

Traditional swaps force users to accept immediate execution and visible slippage conditions. This creates poor UX in volatile markets.

Solution

Xyther lets users express trading outcomes as intents and delegates execution to solver agents through an HCS-based broadcast layer.

Why Hedera

Fast finality, low fees, and predictable consensus ordering via HCS make intent relaying and solver competition practical for retail and API users.
